Classic Macaroni Salad Recipe

Description:

This flavorful macaroni salad is easy to make and is the perfect crowd-pleasing dish to bring to your next picnic or potluck.

Ingredients:

  • Macaroni
  • Condiments
  • Sugar & Vinegar
  • Seasonings
  • Vegetables

Steps:

  1. Cook Macaroni noodles in salted water.
  2. Mix mayonaise, sugar, vinegar, mustard, and seasonings together.
  3. Stir in ther macaroni and remaining ingredients.
  4. Chill in the refrigerator
